Hello there. My name is Gary and I live in Northern Ireland with my wife and three children.

Here's my introduction

Teaching

I've been a freelance online ESL teacher since 2010, working for Learnlight LCC, based in Madrid. I've been teaching students from Europe, the Americas, Asia, and Africa. I've coached and motivated many students to learn English, improved their pronunciation, boosted their confidence in speaking, helped them add more vocabulary, and overall, made them much better speakers and listeners of English.


Previous to my online work, I was an English teacher in Shanghai, China, for over one year. I taught different levels there, specializing mostly in Business English.

Writing

I've written a lot of materials over the years for both learning and testing. I'm the author and producer of the audiobook, Mastering Phrasal Verbs Like a Native English Speaker Volume 1, a 3 hour audiobook (with pdf) designed for busy students who want to learn and master over 100 phrasal verbs in a stressfree way.

Testing

Test writer, Taiwan LTTC. Years ago I wrote several tests at intermediate and advanced level for students in Taiwan

Web developer

I'm currently working on expanding my expertise in Javascript, the most popular programming language behind most of the internet today.
