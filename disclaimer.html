A note about Mastering Phrasal Verbs Like a Native English Speaker Vol 1

What this book is

This book is a grammar-free audio book putting into context 120 phrasal verbs. It's for your listening pleasure, to be listened to anywhere at any time you wish. It's meant for you if you are busy, if you're travelling, or if you are just wanting to relax while listening to English and wanting to increase your familiarity with many phrasal verbs in the process.

The contexts in this book are primarly those of British English.


What this book isn't

This book is not a magic pill that will suddenly solve all your issues with phrasal verbs. It takes listening practice, and ongoing practice, but with that practice, your confidence will indeed grow and your English will improve.
